La’Darius Marshall (born May 09, 1998) is an American Television Personality, All-Star Cheerleading Athlete, and Instagram Star from Florida. He is best known for appearing in Netflix’s popular 2020 docuseries called “Cheer”.

The show recorded the preparation of the Navarro College cheerleaders. The web program documents their training for a national championship. The first season of the hit show had six episodes which premiered on January 08, 2020.

Cheering Career, Netflix Show & Various Appearances

The 21-years-old La’Darius Marshall bagged huge fame after starring as one of the main members of the cheer program at Navarro. It is called Navarro College Cheer and their coach is Monica Aldama.

Furthermore, Greg Whiteley is the creator, executive producer, and director of the project. He is also famous for his work in “Last Chance U” (2016-present).

Monica helped her team in winning various National Championship. Moreover, Marshall’s cheer group is a 14-time NCA National Championship-winning team. They won the title over the past two decades since 2000.

Navarro College Cheer even claimed it’s fifth NCA Grand National Championship since 2012. Some of the main casts of Cheer are Morgan Simianer, Lexi Brumback, Jerry Harris, Gabi Butler, and much more.
