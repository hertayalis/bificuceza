QB1: Is Spencer Rattler albino? Netflix fans want to learn more about his race

June 12, 2024
blog

The Netflix series “QB1: Beyond the Lights” has captured the hearts of audiences for three seasons, and fans are eagerly awaiting a fourth installment in 2020. Among the quarterbacks featured on the show, none have garnered as much attention as Spencer Rattler.

Spencer, the confident QB from season 3, made headlines when he was suspended from his high school’s football season for undisclosed reasons. Now playing for the Oklahoma Sooners, Spencer has gained a whole new fanbase eager to learn more about the quarterback. Here’s what we’ve found out about Spencer Rattler.

Who is Spencer Rattler?

Spencer Rattler is a 19-year-old five-star quarterback originally from Phoenix, Arizona. He held the title of Arizona’s number 1 quarterback while filming “QB1: Beyond the Lights.” Spencer committed to Oklahoma University and officially joined the team in September 2019, having signed his letter of intent in December 2018.

However, to the surprise of many, Spencer did not start for the Oklahoma Sooners in his Freshman year. The top QB position was claimed by Alabama quarterback Jalen Hurts.

 

Is Spencer Rattler Albino?

While it’s unconfirmed, it is unlikely that Spencer Rattler has albinism. Some fans have speculated about this, but there is no official confirmation from Spencer. When examining pictures of his mother, father, and his sister Olivia, who shares a similar hair color and skin tone with Spencer, it seems improbable. Albinism is a genetic condition that can run in families, but having one or more albino children is rare.

Follow Spencer on Instagram

You can find Spencer Rattler on Instagram under the handle @spencer_rattler. With over 296,000 followers and a coveted blue verification tick, Spencer’s Instagram feed is mainly filled with football-related pictures, along with occasional snaps featuring his long-term girlfriend.

Spencer Rattler continues to rise in the world of football, and fans eagerly await his future successes.
