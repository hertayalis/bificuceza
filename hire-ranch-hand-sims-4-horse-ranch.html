How to hire a Ranch Hand in The Sims 4 Horse Ranch

July 14, 2024
blog

If you are getting stuck into the latest expansion in The Sims 4, Horse Ranch, you may be wondering how to hire a Ranch Hand in the game. A Ranch Hand can be incredibly useful as they can give you an extra set of hands at the Ranch.

They can even help you when you are too busy to look after your horses. Let’s get into how you can hire a Ranch Hand in The Sims 4 Horse Ranch.

How to hire a Ranch Hand in The Sims 4 Horse Ranch

In order to hire a Ranch Hand in The Sims 4, you can do this by selecting the Ranch Bulletin Board in Chestnut Ridge, or you can hire them from the services option on your phone. Once you have hired them, they will work from 7 AM to 7 PM. If they work the full day, they will cost a total of 244 Simoleons. 

Managing a farm can be incredibly overwhelming for your sims and they may feel that they need some extra help around the farm, especially if they are the only person working there. It also depends on how many jobs they have to do around the farm.

For example, if they are taking care of animals and plants, it will be exhausting for them to find time to look after themselves. Luckily, this is where hiring a Ranch Hand in the Sims 4 Horse Ranch expansion comes in. 

Ranch Hands can do a variety of jobs such as taking care of crops, repairing items, and taking care of any of the animals on the farm. You can choose what chores they do for you by interacting with the Ranch Hand and choosing their chores in the Assign Chores menu. 

It’s important to note that they won’t collect manure or harvest plants unless you specifically ask them to. If they are asked to do this, the items will appear in the household inventory. 

If you want to fire them or dismiss them, you can do that by interacting with them, or by using your Sim’s phone to dismiss them.

That’s everything you need to know about how to hire a Ranch Hand in The Sims 4 Horse Ranch.
