Beyonce and Blue Ivy's Matching VMA Red Carpet Looks

July 22, 2024

Forget being fashionably late. Beyoncé and Blue Ivy Carter were one of the earliest celebrities to arrive to the VMAs red carpet, and clearly set the bar high.

The mother-daughter duo stunned in matching sparkling looks, with Bey sporting a nearly see-through silvery gown with a statement feathered neckline by Francesco Scognamiglio couture, and Blue in a similarly shimmery dress (with a tutu, of course), nude-pink sneakers, boxer braids and a tiara.
