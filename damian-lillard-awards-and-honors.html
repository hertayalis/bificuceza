Damian Lillard Awards And Honors

Damian Lillard Awards And Honors: Born on July 15, 1990, Damian Lillard, officially known as Damian Lamonte Ollie Lillard Sr. is an American professional basketball player.

He developed a passion for playing basketball at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after American basketball players.

At the time of this writing (Tuesday, June 20, 2023), Lillard is a professional basketball player for the Portland Trail Blazers of the National Basketball Association.

He played college basketball for the Weber State Wildcats and earned third-team All-American honors in 2012

Lillard was voted the 2012 NBA Rookie of the Year after being selected by Portland with the sixth overall pick in the 2012 NBA draft.

As a Portland Trail Blazers player, he has received seven NBA All-Star and seven All-NBA Team selections, the only player in Trail Blazers franchise history to do so.

He won a gold medal on the 2020 U.S. Olympic team in Tokyo. In October 2021, he was honored as one of the league’s greatest players of all time by being named to the NBA 75th Anniversary Team.

Apart from his basketball career, Lillard is a hip-hop artist and rapper by stage name; Dame D.O.L.L.A., which stands for Different On Levels the Lord Allows. He owns his own record label, Front Page Music.

In June 2023, Damian Lillard hit major headlines for being the subject of a multitude of trade rumors since the start of the NBA offseason.

However, according to multiple online reports, the Trail Blazers star wants to remain with Blazers as trade rumors swirl.

Damian Lillard Awards And Honors

Damian Lillard is a recipient of several awards and honors including;

NBA

  • 7× NBA All-Star (2014, 2015, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2023)
  • All-NBA First Team (2018)
  • 4× All-NBA Second Team (2016, 2019, 2020, 2021)
  • 2× All-NBA Third Team (2014, 2023)
  • NBA Bubble Most Valuable Player (2020)
  • NBA Rookie of the Year (2013)
  • NBA All-Rookie First Team (2013)
  • NBA Three-Point Contest champion (2023)
  • J. Walter Kennedy Citizenship Award (2019)
  • NBA Teammate of the Year (2021)
  • NBA Rising Star (2012, 2013)

College

  • AP third-team All-American (2012)
  • NABC third-team All-American (2012)
  • 2× Big Sky Conference Player of the Year (2010, 2012)
  • 3× First-team All-Big Sky (2009, 2010, 2012)
  • 2× Big Sky All-Tournament Team (2010, 2012)
  • Big Sky Freshman of the Year (2009)
  • No. 1 retired by Weber State
