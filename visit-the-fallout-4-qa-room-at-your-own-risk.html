Visit The Fallout 4 QA Room At Your Own Risk

August 02, 2024
blog

Fallout 4 has sent gamers of all platforms into a scavenging and fighting frenzy, as players attempt to acquire the best loot in the game.

There’s nothing more rewarding than clearing a camp of Super Mutants or Raiders and uncovering a plethora of ammo and rare gear inside the compound.

However, Fallout 4 PC gamers have yet another option to test out the best gear that the wasteland has to offer. Hidden inside a secret quality assurance room, PC players can find every Fallout 4 item. Yes, you read that correctly, all the items in the game exist here, and this option can be accessed via the command console.

**Please note: before you attempt the actions shown and described here** Bethesda vice president of PR and marketing, Pete Hines, has stated in a Twitter discussion that use of console commands can corrupt your save files and are not supported by Bethesda.

Spoiler: The YouTube video from Tyrannicon shows us what the inside of the secret room looks like and the entire collection of power armour suits. If this is too much of a spoiler, please do not watch the video.

To access the command console in Fallout 4 press the tilde (~) key on your keyboard. Type "COC QASMOKE" to reach the quality assurance testing room.
